About Us

About Our Lady of Peace
Multipurpose Cooperative

Our Lady of Peace Multipurpose Cooperative (OLPMPC) is one of the hundreds of cooperatives serving the City of Antipolo. It is named after the National Shrine Patroness, the Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage. For 38 years, we have remained committed to empowering lives, strengthening our community, and building a better tomorrow through cooperation, unity, growth, and service.

Mission

Makabuo, maipatupad at maisabuhay ang mga programa, plano at proyekto na naaayon sa panuntunang makatao at maka-Diyos — upang mapabuti ang buhay ng bawat miyembro at komunidad.

Vision

A self-sustaining, God-centered cooperative community where every member actively participates in economic growth, social development, and spiritual well-being — guided by the principles of cooperation and solidarity.

Legal

Data Privacy Statement

(Data Privacy Act of 2012)

General Statement

OLPMPC recognizes the fundamental human right of privacy by protecting your data in accordance with the laws and standards under Republic Act No. 10173, also known as "DATA PRIVACY ACT OF 2012". OLPMPC assures that the data we collect, process, store and share are protected and adhere with the Privacy Principles of transparency, legitimate purpose and proportionality.

Information we collect:

When you apply as a OLPMPC member, employee, contractor, consultant, service provider, and apprentice, or transact and interact as a customer to any establishment, branch, authorized representative of the Cooperative, we collect your personal information. This may include the following:

  • Name, contact details, address, birthdate, age, marital status, gender, education, biometrics;
  • Specimen signatures, copy of government ID's, employment details, emails, social media accounts;
  • Financial information, employment history;
  • CCTV footage when visiting our OLPMPC office.

Data / Information Usage

Your information will be used to validate and process applications and transactions with our business operations and offices such as follows:

  • Membership, Loans, Savings and Time Deposit applications
  • Records purchases and charge transactions
  • Application for employment, contractors and service providers
  • Receive info for any updates about marketing activities and promos
  • Share with third parties Personal Information Processors (PIPs) bound with data sharing agreement

Store and Protect

OLPMPC assures that the information we collect is stored in a secured location, and only authorized personnel can access. Access to electronic information is bound with policy and non-disclosure agreement with issued security clearance.

Retention and Disposal

Your personal information shall be kept for as long as you do business with the cooperative and subject for disposal through deletion and shredding in accordance with the law and internal policy.

Members Rights

In compliance with the Republic Act 10173, we respect and recognize your rights to be informed, access your records, object, request for correction or blocking, damages, file a complaint, rectify your information, and data portability, provided that those will not affect the delivery of our services on your transactions with the cooperative. For comments, suggestions, and clarification, feel free to contact us.

Become a Member

Joining OLPMPC is a step toward financial security and community growth. Open to all qualified individuals in Antipolo and surrounding areas. Choose how you'd like to apply:

  • ✓ Must be a resident or worker in Antipolo City or nearby areas
  • ✓ Must attend the Pre-Membership Education Seminar (PMES)
  • ✓ Must be at least 18 years old and of legal capacity
  • ✓ Submit valid ID and completed membership application form
  • ✓ Pay the required membership fees and initial share capital
  1. Visit the OLPMPC office in Antipolo City during walk-in application hours.
  2. Attend the on-site Pre-Membership Education Seminar (PMES).
  3. Fill out the membership application form provided at the office.
  4. Submit a valid ID and your completed application form.
  5. Pay the required membership fees and initial share capital at the cashier to complete enrollment.

Characteristics of a Member

A cooperative member is more than just a customer — they are an active owner and participant. Here are the defining characteristics of an OLPMPC member:

🙋
Voluntary Participation

Membership is open and voluntary to all eligible individuals without discrimination.

🗳️
Democratic Control

Each member has one vote, regardless of their share capital contribution.

📊
Economic Participation

Members contribute equitably to, and democratically control, the capital of their cooperative.

🤝
Cooperative Education

Members are required to attend the Pre-Membership Education Seminar (PMES) and are encouraged to participate in ongoing cooperative education.

🌱
Community Concern

Members work for the sustainable development of their communities through policies approved by their cooperatives.

🤲
Cooperation Among Cooperatives

Members serve their members most effectively by working together through local, national, and international structures.
